We are pleased to introduce ourselves as one of the leading Electrical Contractors in South India holding Hight Tension License (HT) for the state of Kerala. Over the time period, we have asssociated with major business sectors and successfully carried out execution of various turnkey electrical projects with topmost quality. Our Spectrum of work spreads across the areas from estimation to completion.

Whether you require domestic, industrial, or commercial electrical work, Electreus Engineers are fully committed to providing professional and affordable electrical services. Electreus Engineers is a client-centric organization, and by serving our clients in the most appropriate manner, we look forward to consistently expanding our client base. Through our hard work and sheer commitment, today our organization has become the electrical service provider of choice for several areas in and outside Kerala.
